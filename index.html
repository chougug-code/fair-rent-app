About FairRent

Your AI-powered tenant protection shield

🔍 How It Works

  • Upload a photo or PDF of your lease
  • Our AI reads every clause in detail
  • We cross-check against your city's current laws
  • Get a risk score + actionable steps

⚡ 2026 City Law Sync

FairRent queries real-time legal databases to ensure your analysis reflects the latest local tenant protection laws, rent control caps, and just-cause eviction rules — even if they were passed last month.

🚩 What We Check

  • Illegal late fee amounts
  • Security deposit over legal cap
  • Missing landlord entry notice
  • Illegal lease waivers
  • Rent increase violations
  • Retaliation clauses
  • Hidden or unlabeled fees

⚠️ Disclaimer

FairRent provides informational analysis only and is not legal advice. For serious lease issues, consult a licensed tenant attorney or your local tenant rights organization.
